You are what you eat. As glitter becomes a growing trend in rave culture the environment is suffering. The sparkle and glits gets washed away in the shower and makes its wake to the ocean where fish inhale it. The glitter is so tiny that it is also able to make its way through water filters and into our drinking water. At the end of the day we end up consuming the same glitter that completed our concert look.
